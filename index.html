Qamar Privacy Policy

Your Islamic Companion Discord Bot

Welcome to Qamar, your dedicated Islamic companion bot for Discord. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

About Qamar

Qamar is a Discord bot designed to enrich your Islamic experience with features including:

🕌 Prayer Times Daily prayer schedules
📖 Quran Verses Sacred Quranic content
📿 Dhikr Counter Track your remembrance
⏰ Reminders Important notifications

Information We Collect

User-Provided Information

  • Discord User ID - Used to identify and personalize your experience
  • Location - Stored only when you voluntarily set it for prayer time calculations
  • Prayer Time Preferences - Your selected calculation method and time adjustments
  • Dhikr Counter Data - Your personal dhikr tracking and counters
  • Reminder Preferences - Commands you've set up for notifications

Automatically Collected Information

  • Timestamps of bot interactions
  • Commands executed (for improvement purposes only)
  • Server/Guild ID (only when bot is invited)

Data Storage & Security

We take data security seriously. Here's how your information is handled:

Local Storage Format

User data is stored in a users.json file maintained locally on our secure servers. This file contains:

  • Your Discord User ID (non-personally identifiable)
  • Your configured location for prayer times
  • Your prayer time preferences
  • Dhikr counter statistics
  • Reminder configurations

Important: This data is never shared, exported, or used for any purpose other than providing Qamar's services.

Encryption & Protection

  • Data is stored securely with appropriate access controls
  • Only the bot owner can access the users.json file
  • Regular backups ensure data integrity
  • No unauthorized third parties have access to your information

Third-Party APIs

Qamar integrates with the following external services to provide its features:

🕌 Aladhan API

Used for retrieving accurate prayer times based on your location.

  • Only your location (when you set it) is sent to this API
  • Prayer time data is provided in real-time
  • No personal identifiers are sent

📖 AlQuran Cloud API

Provides authentic Quran verses and Islamic content.

  • No personal data is shared with this service
  • Only requests for Quranic content are made
  • All content is retrieved publicly available information

Note: These are well-established Islamic APIs with their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them at their respective websites.

How We Use Your Data

  • Prayer Times: Display accurate prayer schedules based on your location
  • Quran Verses: Retrieve and share Islamic content when requested
  • Dhikr Counter: Track and maintain your personal dhikr statistics
  • Reminders: Send you notifications based on your configured preferences
  • Bot Improvement: Anonymized usage patterns help us improve the bot

✓ We never sell, trade, or share your data with third parties
✓ Your data is used exclusively for providing Qamar's services
✓ We don't use your information for advertising or marketing

Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

View Your Data

Use the `/data` command to see what information Qamar has stored about you.

Delete Your Data

Use the `/delete-data` command to completely remove all your information from our systems. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Update Your Information

You can update your location, preferences, and settings at any time using Qamar's configuration commands.

Opt-Out of Reminders

Disable any reminder or notification features you don't wish to use.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as you use Qamar. Here's our retention policy:

  • Active Users: Data is maintained as long as you use the bot
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be removed after 6 months of inactivity (with prior notice)
  • On Request: You can request immediate deletion at any time using the `/delete-data` command

Children's Privacy

Qamar is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete such information and terminate the child's account.

Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure local storage with restricted access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Discord API security best practices
  • No storage of sensitive data (passwords, tokens, etc.)
  • Immediate response to any security incidents
